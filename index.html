How it works for creators

Turn your content into income with our revolutionary token system

Post Content

Post your content just like on other social networks. Share videos, photos, music, or text that expresses your creativity.

Earn Gems

When others interact with your content through likes, comments, and shares, you earn Gems in real-time.

Convert Gems

Every 24 hours, your Gems automatically convert to Peer Tokens. These aren’t just points, they have real monetary value.

Cash Out

Exchange Peer Tokens for real money via bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency. Your creativity pays off.
