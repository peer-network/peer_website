How Peer Network Works

Content creators post to attract viewers, who engage with content through likes, comments and views. This engagement generates Gems for creators, which convert to tokens that have real value.

Earn Gems for interactions

Your content automatically generates Gems when others engage with it.
More engagement equals more earnings.

Daily Interactions

Each user gets a limited number of free daily actions (likes, comments) that reset at 00:00 UTC. Views are unlimited, and you can make one free post per day.

Additional Posts

Want to post more? Each additional post costs 20 Tokens. Disliking content costs 5 Tokens per dislike.

Gems for actions

Every 24 hours at 00:00 UTC, your earned Gems convert to Peer Tokens based on your share of the day's total engagement.

Token Distribution

96% goes directly to content creators

2% goes to Peer Network
1% is burned (removed from circulation)

1% is allocated to the liquidity pool

Cash out anytime

Our liquidity pool lets you exchange Peer Tokens for Euro whenever you want, with transparent rates determined by supply and demand.

How Our LP Works

The liquidity pool holds both Peer Tokens and BTC (approximately €10,000 worth). When you sell tokens, they enter the pool, BTC is converted to fiat, and the amount is sent to your bank account. Token prices adjust dynamically based on pool reserves.

Token Burn Mechanism

When tokens are bought and burned, they are removed from circulation, reducing supply and potentially increasing value. However, the main driver of token value is that 98% of money spent on ads or interactions goes into the liquidity pool (LP), raising the $ amount in the LP each time an action or ad is purchased.
