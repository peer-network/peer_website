Questions and Answers

We believe in transparency. Here are answers to common questions about our platform. If you need more help, our team is ready to assist.

What happens if I want to withdraw my tokens?

Our blockchain system tracks interactions immutably, letting you verify earnings in real-time as likes, comments, and shares convert to Gems and Peer Tokens.

How do I join Peer Network?

Getting started is easy: download our app or visit the web platform, create an account, and start posting. Earn Gems as users interact with your content, converting to Peer Tokens daily.

How you ensure my earnings are fair?

Our blockchain system tracks interactions immutably, letting you verify earnings in real-time as likes, comments, and shares convert to Gems and Peer Tokens.

How many posts can I make per day?

You are allowed to make one post per day at no cost. However, any additional posts made on the same day will incur a charge of 20 Peer Tokens each.
